# People of the Aster Program

Dr. Mara Voss is the founding director of the Aster Program.
A former navigation lead at the Calloway Flight Center, she is known for insisting that every mission share hardware or data with at least one other
mission, a policy nicknamed the Voss Weave.

Ines Okonkwo is the program's senior flight director.
She commanded the first expedition to Kestrel Station, co-authored the Unified Operations Charter, and brokered the test stand agreement at Port
Halcyon.

Dr. Sefa Lindqvist is principal investigator of the Corona Imaging Array on the Helia Probe and chairs the Farsight telescope allocation committee.
Her early interference findings reshaped the IPX-9 power bus design.

Rohan Mehta is the program's best-known engineer.
He leads the Hearthfire engine team at Port Halcyon, designed the quiet power bus used across the fleet, flew on the first Kestrel Station expedition,
and performed the docking adapter spacewalk.

Dr. Amara Sole leads life-support and astrochemistry.
Her team built the Greenloop bioreactor on Kestrel Station and the Wetlab assays aboard the Talos Lander.
